Vision

ヴィジョン

Our vision at ERamen is to redefine the ramen experience, elevating it to new heights of culinary artistry and cultural fusion. We envision ERamen as a global phenomenon that not only satisfies the taste buds but also captivates the hearts and minds of ramen enthusiasts worldwide.

Mission

ミッション

We strive to serve exceptional ramen dishes that delight our customers. Using organic flour and quality ingredients, we craft each bowl with care, ensuring a perfect balance of flavors and textures. We also honor the authentic techniques and flavors of traditional ramen, preserving its rich heritage while incorporating regional influences. We aim to offer a genuine and true-to-origins ramen experience.

History

歴史

1910

ERamen is an illustrious and esteemed establishment, boasting an extraordinary legacy as one of the oldest ramen shops in the entire world. Its rich history can be traced back to its inception in the year 1910, when it first opened its doors in Japan, captivating the hearts and palates of ramen enthusiasts with its exquisite culinary offerings.

2015

Recognizing the immense popularity and global demand for their delectable creations, ERamen expanded its horizons and ventured into the vibrant Indonesian culinary landscape in 2015, bringing with it a century-old tradition of crafting unrivaled ramen experiences. A true testament to their commitment to quality and authenticity, ERamen diligently employs the use of organic flour in the meticulous preparation of their renowned ramen noodles.
